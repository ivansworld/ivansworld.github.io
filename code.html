add as many as are applicable.

Appearance

Clothing Across the nerd spectrum is a wide variety of attire. Choose the option below that fits (haha) you the best.
D+++ = Very conservative dress; usually a suit (and/or vest) and tie.
D++ = A good button-up shirt and a nice pair of slacks/khakis.
D+ = A nice polo or t-shirt with khakis or jeans.
D = Nothing too fancy, just a t-shirt and jeans.
D- = A tanktop with jeans or shorts... and flip-flops.
D-- = Punk clothing, piercings, and prominent tattoos.

DX = I'm a cross dresser
D? = Most of the time, I don't even know what I'm wearing.
D! = No clothing, no problem!
DUW = I wear the same dirty, unwashed clothes every single day.
