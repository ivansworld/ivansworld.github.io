Possible Outcomes

0-16 points = Normal
You're like most of the population, perfectly normal. Contrary to what is
spread around today, being normal is a blessing and is a good thing to be.

15-29 points = Dork
Some could say that your interests are... odd. Despite being cursed with
social ineptness, unlike the nerds you didn't get the academics along with it. Still, people
find you to be quite comedic and funny, albeit eccentric.


30-45 points = Geek
Although you may seem to be pretty social and relatively normal on the
outside, you spend a great deal of time obsessing about and consuming media related to your
favorite video games and super heroes. Unlike nerds and dorks, you have no
problem with socialization and, in fact, enjoy going to conventions and the like.


46-60 points = Nerd
You are somebody who enjoys spending your time sharpening your academic
skills and learning more about the world around you. While the geeks are out socializing
and the dorks are picking their noses, you are busy programming or arranging
your calculator collection.
