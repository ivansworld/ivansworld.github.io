Frequently Asked Questions

What is the point of this website?
Ivan's World does not aim to serve a single function. Rather, it is an experiment
to see how closely I could reproduce the "90s web page" look on an entire website. Since
then, I've transformed it into a personal website where I can upload all of my content to.

Why am I here?
You either found one of "advertisements" somewhere in the city, came through a link
on one of my other accounts, were referred here by me in real life, or were summoned by
a wizard.

This website looks terrible!
Then go ahead, make your own. You have nothing to prove to me. I do not care.

Why "Ivan?"
Ivan was my old screen name on a popular chat client. When deciding on the website
domain name, I wanted it to incorporate the alias, in case somebody was referred from
the Ivan account.

It wouldn't hurt to quit being so mysterious and use your real name.
I like separating my online life from the real world. Privacy is nice, especially
in this day and age where everybody's trying to track you.

What is there to do?
What is there not do do? A good place to start would be at the site Index. I'd
suggest checking out my opinions page if you like reading differing viewpoints. There
are also a few interesting downloads, such as a poorly written sci-fi novella which I
made some time ago, on the personal page. Feel free to bookmark this website, as more
content is bound to come in the future.

Whoa, what happened to the old site?
I see that you noticed the recent change in theming, long-time user. Doesn't it look
slightly better? I saved a copy of the old site to my computer, in case you're wondering.
Still, it's not coming back.
